Our mission is to serve the best pizza in town!

Sal's opened in New York in 1937, the current owner (Sallie) is the great grandson of the original Salvatore Rico.
We still follow the same receipes, passed down through the ages.
Only the freshes toppings are used, we bake with the finest of flour to make our pizza dough,
and we still use the same brick ovens. Stop in for some of the best food in town.
